Regulatory compliance

Your regulators
already require this

Argentine and international regulations increasingly mandate documented penetration testing. We help you comply — and prove it.

Financial sector
BCRA A 8398

Banks & Fintechs

Requires documented penetration testing for all entities under Central Bank supervision. Non-compliance risks operating license.

Capital markets
CNV RG 939

Brokers & ALyC

General Resolution 939/2022 mandates cybersecurity plans with technical testing for all capital market participants.

E-commerce & payments
PCI-DSS v4.0

Card processing

Annual pentesting required for any business processing credit or debit cards. Mandated by Visa, Mastercard, and Amex.

Data & privacy
Ley 25.326

Any company with data

Technical security measures required for all personal data processors. A breach without prior testing means direct civil liability.

Public sector
Decreto 1390/2023

Government entities

CNC and Resolution JGM 1523/2019 mandate NIST framework compliance across the National Public Administration.

SaaS / International
SOC 2 · ISO 27001

Companies selling abroad

SOC 2 and ISO 27001 — required by US and EU enterprise clients — mandate pentesting as a core technical control.
